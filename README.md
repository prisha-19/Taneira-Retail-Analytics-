Key Findings


High return rate (25%) with mostly unspecified reasons — opportunity to improve return-reason tracking
Revenue is heavily concentrated in Sarees, Unstitched Suit Sets, and Kurtas
Stock levels don't always align with revenue contribution — signals of potential overstocking
Out-of-stock SKUs still showing demand — restocking opportunity
