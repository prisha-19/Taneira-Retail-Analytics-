# Retail Analytics & Inventory Dashboard — Taneira (Titan Company Ltd)

Sales, returns, product intelligence, and inventory health analytics built in Excel and Power BI, using real retail business data from Taneira.

**Intern:** Prisha Chakraborty, B.Tech Mechanical Engineering, IIT (ISM) Dhanbad
**Duration:** May 2026 – June 2026

---

## Overview

During my first-year internship at Titan Company Ltd (Taneira), I analyzed retail and stock data to build interactive dashboards that turn raw business data into actionable insights — covering sales performance, inventory health, product categorization, and operational status across multiple sales channels.

## Objectives

- Monitor sales performance and inventory health
- Categorize products and analyze return status
- Track product arrivals and stock status
- Identify products with sales despite being out-of-stock
- Build interactive dashboards with key KPIs

## Tools Used

**Excel** — Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, VLOOKUP/XLOOKUP, formulas
**Power BI** — Power Query, data modeling & relationships, DAX, drill-downs
**SQL** — data extraction and analysis

## Dashboards

### Excel Dashboard — Sales, Discounts & Inventory
- Saree contributes ₹2.44 Cr (67% of total revenue)
- WCS channel drove 86% of total discounts; average discount 15%
- Lehenga is the fastest-moving product category
- Average inventory per product: ~3 units

## Key Findings

- High return rate (25%) with mostly unspecified reasons — opportunity to improve return-reason tracking
- Revenue is heavily concentrated in Sarees, Unstitched Suit Sets, and Kurtas
- Stock levels don't always align with revenue contribution — signals of potential overstocking
- Out-of-stock SKUs still showing demand — restocking opportunity

## Challenges Faced

- Handling duplicate inventory values from merged datasets
- Building accurate relationships across multiple tables
- Validating KPI definitions and calculations
- Cleaning and understanding large, real-world datasets

## Learning Outcomes

Data cleaning, data visualization, business analytics, Power BI & Excel dashboarding, SQL querying, and problem-solving with real business data.
